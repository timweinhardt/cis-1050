Construction Beginning on New Location in Waterloo, ON

After many long weeks of planning and preparation, we're excited to announce the construction of a new Burger Bros location in Waterloo, Ontario. This free standing restaurant is the chain's second brick-and-mortar location and first drive-thru. Burger Bros co-founder Tim Weinhardt shares, "We couldn't be more thrilled to serve the community of Waterloo". The grand opening of this location is planned for Summer 2024.
