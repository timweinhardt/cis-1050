About Us

Learn what our company is all about, why it was started and what it takes to keep it running.

Who are we?

We’re more than just a burger restaurant. At Burger Bros it’s our mission to provide a remarkable experience to our customers with industry-leading quality and care. We live by the saying, “quality over quantity”, which is why we’ve chosen to keep our menu small, but incredibly delicious! With a focus on quick service, we strive to serve our customers as quickly and efficiently as possible. Whether you’re looking to grab a quick bite after work, celebrate a friend’s birthday or merely a hungry burger-lover, Burger Bros has got you covered.

Our History

The first Burger Bros hamburger left the grill in August 2021. At the time, two teenage brothers—Tim and Chris Weinhardt—were navigating the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic. Born, raised and living in Kitchener, Ontario, the two had never experienced anything to the likes of the pandemic. As was the case with most people around the world, their summer was spent in isolation while the country locked down to prevent the spread of infectious disease. While infection rates were rising, so was the boredom in the Weinhardt household. It was a combination of their dwindling motivation and yearning to see family and friends that sparked the beginning of a great idea.

The two brothers decided to make free meals for their friends and family. Over the next few weeks, they were hard at work gathering ingredients and arranging deliveries for their friends. What was the goal? To brighten someone else's day. Both Tim and Chris understood the challenging circumstances that the pandemic had put their closest friends in, and they felt that this was a small (but impactful) way that they could make a difference. That summer, the brothers served a total of 7 different families.

In the following years, Tim and Chris began working on plans to bring Burger Bros to real customers. Up until this point, the entire operation was completely non-profit. The two decided to open their very own food truck and attended local events in Kitchener. Word spread quickly about the small burger restaurant, and online reviews praised the brothers for their high quality food and service. As the opportunity for investments arose, the very first brick and mortar location opened on April 12, 2023, in Guelph, Ontario.

Today, the team at Burger Bros is quickly expanding. With 3 new locations in development, our team at Burger Bros is hard at work to bring our restaurants to more locations across the country. As we grow in scale, our commitment to providing customers with an exceptional experience is growing as well. The story of Burger Bros is not over, in fact, it has merely begun. We're so glad to have you along for this journey and hope that we'll always need to come back to update this page.
