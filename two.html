Future Locations

When selecting a new location for a Burger Bros restaurant, there’s a lot happens behind the scenes. It’s our goal to make Burger Bros available to every single Canadian—and eventually the rest of the world. To make this a reality, it's in our best interest to find the right community for each new location. Currently, our focus is on expansion in Ontario. By 2029, we plan on opening 15 new locations in the province and our first out-of-province location by 2030. To give you a sneak peek of things to come, here are some of our plans for the near future.

Here are our latest plans, as of March 2024:

  • 1 new location in Waterloo, Ontario (Estimated completion, Summer 2024)
  • 2 new locations in Mississauga, Ontario (Estimated completion, early 2025)

As we expand our reach, we would love to hear your feedback as well. If you are an independent business owner interested in pursuing a future as a Burger Bros owner/operator, please reach out to us at (123) 456-7890. If you are a passionate fan and would like to see Burger Bros in your hometown, leave us an email at support@burgerbros.com. We want to thank all of you for your continued support and enthusiasm, and we hope to serve you soon!
